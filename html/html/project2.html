The Boston Red sox are one of the most iconic and historic teams in Major league Baseball. They have won multiple World Series Champions and have a strong fanbase.

The Boston Red Sox play at Fenway park and is home to the iconic "Green Monster". Fenway Park is also one of the oldest stadiums in Baseball.
